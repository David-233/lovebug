Obrumankoma, Odapagyan, Oson

Obrumankoma, Odapagyan and Oson (the Whale, the Eagle and the Elephant respectively)are three great Fante warriors.According to tradition and history, Obrumankoma and Odapagyan died on this exodus and were embalmed and carried the rest of the way.

The Fante people mainly loacted at the coastal areas of central and Western regions of Ghana.Their land streches from the eastern part of western region in the Gomoa.Over the last lastb half century,[as of?] Fante communities have been established as far as Gambia ,Libaria ,Angola and more due to fishing expenditinss. [citation needed] Major Fante cities and towns in modern Ghana includes Cape Coast, Saltpon, Elmina, Sekondi-Takoradi, Agona, Mankessim, Winneba, Shama and Anomabo.
